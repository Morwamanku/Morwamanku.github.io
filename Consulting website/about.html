OUR JOURNEY

Mannya Consulting is a proudly South African, black-owned consulting firm specialising in civil engineering, architectural design, project management, quantity surveying, and urban and regional planning. Founded by Tshegofatso Mamotheti, a qualified civil engineering professional with a passion for infrastructure development and community upliftment, the firm was established to offer technically sound, contextually relevant, and forward-thinking solutions within the built environment.
