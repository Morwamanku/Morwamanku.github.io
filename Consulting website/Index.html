ACSA Project

Mannya consulting was appointed by Airports Company of South Africa (ACSA) for engineering services for stormwater designs and platform designs for the newly proposed ground mount 750 KWP solar PV plant at east London Airport, we successfully delivered our first civil engineering project, demonstrating our commitment to quality and client satisfaction.

Kentia Place Project

Completion of Kentia Place housing renovations (2024) Expanded our portfolio with the successful delivery of Kentia housing renovation, his included professional architectural, civil, quantify survey and structural engineering services for the housing extension of 200m2 , underscoring our ability to manage complex projects with efficiency and precision.

Meet the Visionary Founder Behind Mannya Consulting: Guiding Excellence in Engineering

Tshegofatso Mannya Mamotheti

Our founder, a young and ambitious leader, embarked on his journey in engineering at Tshwane University of Technology, where he earned his degree in Civil Engineering. With a passion for innovation and a drive for excellence, he quickly established himself in the consulting field, gaining invaluable experience along the way. Armed with a deep understanding of engineering principles and a keen eye for detail, he leads Mannya Consulting with a vision to surpass industry standards and redefine excellence in the field. Committed to client satisfaction and continuous improvement, our founder inspires our team to push boundaries and deliver innovative solutions for every project we undertake.
