What is Mannya consulting

Mannya Consulting is a dynamic multi-disciplinary infrastructure consulting firm serving both private and public sector clients.

We provide integrated, end-to-end solutions to meet diverse infrastructure requirements. Our expert team brings together specialists from across the built environment, including architects, environmental consultants, town planners, project managers, quantity surveyors, and electrical, industrial, mechanical, civil, municipal, and structural engineers. This collective expertise enables us to deliver innovative, efficient, and sustainable outcomes for every project.
